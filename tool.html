Key Findings

  • The proposed price increase of 15% shows a strong likelihood (84%) of increasing revenue.
  • The median projected revenue increase is 23.4% over the next 12 months.
  • There is a 12% chance of customer churn exceeding 15%, which could negatively impact long-term revenue.
  • The simulation has a 94% confidence level, indicating high reliability in the projections.

Detailed Recommendation

Based on the simulation results, we recommend:

  1. Proceed with the price increase of 15% as it shows strong positive revenue impact.
  2. Implement a customer loyalty program concurrently to mitigate potential churn risk.
  3. Monitor key metrics closely for the first 90 days, particularly customer retention and repeat purchase rates.
  4. Consider A/B testing the price increase with a segment of customers before full rollout if risk tolerance is low.

Next Steps:
