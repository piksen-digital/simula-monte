Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about SimulaNegocio.

What data do I need to use SimulaNegocio?

You can start with simple CSV files of historical business data - sales records, inventory levels, customer transactions, or operational metrics. The system automatically identifies patterns and key variables. No data cleaning or preprocessing is required.

How accurate are the simulations?

SimulaNegocio uses Monte Carlo methods, the industry standard for business simulations. Each simulation runs thousands of scenarios based on your actual historical data and market variables. Every result includes a confidence score so you understand the reliability of projections.

Is technical expertise required?

No. SimulaNegocio is designed for business users, not data scientists. The interface guides you through setting up scenarios in plain business language. If you can use a spreadsheet, you can use SimulaNegocio.

How does this compare to enterprise simulation tools?

Enterprise tools like AnyLogic or Cosmo Tech require specialized training and cost tens of thousands annually. SimulaNegocio delivers 80% of the functionality for 10% of the cost, specifically optimized for SMB use cases like pricing, inventory, and route optimization.

What's included in the free trial?

The 14-day free trial includes full access to all simulation features, 100 simulation runs, and support for datasets up to 10,000 rows. No credit card is required to start your trial.
